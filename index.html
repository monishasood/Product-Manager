ABOUT ME

Operator. Builder. Strategist.

Transforming complex challenges into elegant solutions through data-driven strategy and user-centered design.

I'm a Product Manager who thrives at the intersection of business insight and execution. With over 6 years of experience spanning operations, strategy, and product development, I've learned that great products aren't just about features—they're about solving real problems for real people.

My journey began in operations, where I developed a deep appreciation for process efficiency and the importance of getting details right. I led the operational excellence for 200+ real estate transactions, saving $50K per transaction through data-driven pricing strategies. This operational foundation taught me that sustainable product success requires understanding the entire value chain.

As I transitioned into product management, I brought that operator's mindset with me. At Flyhomes, I led end-to-end product development that boosted team efficiency by 68% and customer satisfaction by 32%. I learned to navigate ambiguity, balance competing stakeholder needs, and ship products that users actually wanted—not just what we thought they needed.

Currently, I'm driving product strategy at SecondWind Pro, where I led the MVP launch of an NIL Valuation Platform from 0 to 1. I built and managed a cross-functional team of 8 across data, engineering, and fundraising, enabling verified profiles for 25+ Division 1 athletes in the first month while supporting $500K+ in active fundraising. I designed automation systems that reduced manual work by 70%, proving that great product thinking scales impact exponentially.

I'm pursuing my MBA at the Wisconsin School of Business, specializing in Technology Strategy and Product Management. This experience has sharpened my strategic thinking and expanded my toolkit for building products that matter.

My Product Philosophy

Build with empathy, execute with precision, iterate with humility. Great products emerge from understanding users deeply, shipping fast, and learning continuously.

What Drives Me

I love the challenge of taking ambiguous problems and transforming them into clear product strategies. Whether it's launching an MVP or optimizing complex workflows, I'm energized by impact.
